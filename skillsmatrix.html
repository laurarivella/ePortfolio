Skills Matrix and Action Plan

This working version of the skills matrix is design to reflect on the skills that:

  • I had before starting the course and I wish to apply to the topics we have studies or will study.
  • I did not have before starting the course and I wish to know more about.
  • I found to have applications in cybersecurity.
  • I found are of interest to potential employers.

Given the amount of work that improving in each subject entails, it would be optimal to start with the skills that score high on the interest rating. Finding real world and cybersecurity applications of those subjects is key to actually improve, otherwise there are too many subjects and commitments which would push these subjects lower on the priority list.
